I am an AI solutions architect focused on healthcare and life sciences, with 6+ years of experience building production AI systems across enterprise environments. My work sits at the intersection of agentic AI, biomedical data, regulated infrastructure, and product strategy.

I have led technical discovery, solution scoping, and deployment for HIPAA/SOC2-conscious AI platforms across pharmaceutical, biotech, and academic medical center customers. Recent work includes agentic research assistants, knowledge graphs, virtual cell and foundation model workflows, EHR-derived cohort discovery, target validation, and evaluation systems for groundedness and tool use.

## What I Build

- Agentic research assistants using planner, retrieval, tool-use, critic, and evaluation loops.
- Clinical and omics pipelines that connect EHR-derived data with molecular datasets through FHIR/OMOP-aware design and terminology normalization.
- LLM evaluation workflows using rubric-based scoring, LLM-as-judge, prompt versioning, red-team sets, and groundedness metrics.
- GPU-aware model deployment and optimization for large biomedical models across cloud, HPC, and on-prem environments.
